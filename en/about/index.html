In spare time, I love photography, video games, and cats.

Below is a picture of myself taken with a Canon EOS 550D, but now I'm using a Fujifilm X-T5. I love the triple-dial design of Fujifilm X-T series, which is both asthetically pleasing and offers convenient control for users.

The Chinese characters on the mirror are 「锲qiè而ér不bù舍shě，金jīn石shí可kě镂lòu」, which means "If you persevere with chiseling, even metal and stone can be engraved". Although this phrase is often overused or even abused, it still highlights the importance of perseverance.
